Applesauce Jello

Applesauce Jello is a lesser-known biproduct from the Jello craze of the 1970. It combines applesauce with typically red jello into a textured, slimey dessert.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz red gelatin
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 1/2 cup cooled applesauce

Steps

  1. Pour gelatin into boiling water in a big bowl.
  2. Add Applesauce.
  3. Stir until properly mixed.
  4. Pour into a large dish and cover with a lid.
  5. Refridgerate until set.
