Mac N Cheese

Macaroni and Cheese, Mac for short, is a simplistic twist on a typical Italian dish. It was first introduced to the U.S. and the U.K. in the 1700's, but the recipe spread even more during the two world wars. Since then it's become a staple on kid's menus and a popular side dish for family get-togethers.

Ingredients

  • macaroni noodles
  • butter
  • milk
  • flour
  • shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta, then drain the excess water.
  2. Mix the milk, butter, and flower in a bowl.
  3. Cook the sauce until it thickens.
  4. Stir in the shredded cheese.
  5. Combine the sauce with the cooked noodles.
  6. Add the pasta to a baking dish. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the pasta.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes at 350degF.
